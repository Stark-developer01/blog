Geordieland

  • Client: Newcastle Gateshead Initiative
  • Date: June 2013
  • Website: Geordieland

Geordieland was an ambitious task, put to Drummond Central by the Newcastle Gateshead Initiative. We wanted to take the interactive city map and push it a bit further.

Using the locations of Tweets sent down the Twitter firehose, we plotted them on a hand-drawn map of the city. This allowed users to see what was happening, and where, in real time.

On top of this, you could get information about key areas of the city. Shopping hot-spots, places to eat, things to do, etc. All the data came from Twitter and the pre-existing Newcastle Gateshead website.
